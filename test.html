Ouzoud Waterfalls

Ouzoud Waterfalls are one of the most famous waterfalls in Morocco, a destination for tourists from inside and outside the country all year round. The waterfalls are located in the village of Tanajmilt, affiliated with the city of Azilal in the High Atlas Mountains, about 161 kilometers from Marrakech. The Ouzoud region is characterized by a healthy climate and picturesque green areas with a variety of plants and animals.

Ouzoud Waterfalls

The Ouzoud Waterfalls are a popular day trip destination from Marrakech, offering visitors a refreshing escape from the city's heat and noise. The falls are accessible by car or bus, with parking available near the entrance to the site. From there, visitors can follow a well-marked trail that leads down to the base of the falls, passing by a series of viewpoints that offer stunning panoramas of the cascades and surrounding landscape.

About This Trip

Experience the breathtaking Ouzoud Waterfalls, one of Morocco's natural wonders. Located in the Atlas Mountains, these 110-meter cascades offer spectacular views and a refreshing escape from the bustling cities.

Our guided tour takes you through olive groves (the name "Ouzoud" means "olive" in Berber) and down to the base of the falls where you can take a refreshing dip in the natural pools. Keep an eye out for the local Barbary macaques that often visit the area!

Trip Highlights

  • Witness the spectacular 110-meter waterfall cascades
  • Enjoy a guided hike through olive groves and lush vegetation
  • Opportunity to swim in natural pools at the base of the falls
  • Spot local wildlife including Barbary macaques
  • Traditional Moroccan lunch with panoramic views (optional)
  • Round-trip transportation from Marrakech included

This day trip from Marrakech is perfect for nature lovers and photographers. The journey takes approximately 2.5 hours each way, giving you ample time to explore the falls and surrounding area.
